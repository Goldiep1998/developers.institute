About the domestic cats

They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines. They are often valuved by humans for companionship.

There are more than seventy cat breds recognized by the various cat registries

Cat senses

  • They can hear sound too faint or to high in frequency for human ears
  • The can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans

The lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests.
It is usually more diurnal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active in night and at
twilight. A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides very in size and
composition from three to 20 adult lions, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females cooperate when
hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe
